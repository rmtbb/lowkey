About Us

The go-to source for top-quality window tinting in the Coachella Valley

Lowkey Window Tint is a 100% locally owned and operated window tinting service in the Coachella Valley. Our business was founded by a man who was passionate about cars and wanted to provide high-quality window tinting to his community. As the business grew, customer service remained a top priority. We believe that every customer deserves the best possible experience, from the initial consultation to the final installation. Our team of expert technicians, led by the founder, takes great care to ensure that every job is completed to the highest standards, with attention to detail and a focus on customer satisfaction.

One of the things that sets Lowkey Window Tint apart is our commitment to using only the best materials and techniques. We believe that window tinting is more than just a cosmetic upgrade - it's an investment in the protection and comfort of your car. That's why we use only top-quality window film that is specifically designed to block out heat and glare, protect against UV rays, and enhance the appearance of your car. We offer a wide range of options for shades, colors, and finishes to suit your style and needs. In addition to providing window tinting services for cars, we also offer tinting for businesses. We understand that the appearance and energy efficiency of a commercial building is important, and we are here to help. Our team has the expertise and equipment to handle even the largest window tinting projects, and we always strive to exceed our customers' expectations.

In the end, what makes Lowkey Window Tint truly special is our passion for what we do. We love cars and we love helping our customers enjoy all the benefits that window tinting has to offer. Whether you're looking to protect your car's interior, stay cool in the summer, or enhance the appearance and energy efficiency of your business, we are here to help. Contact us today to learn more and book your appointment. One of the things that sets Lowkey Window Tint apart is the personal touch that the founder brings to every job. He has a deep understanding of the intricacies of window tinting and is always willing to go the extra mile to ensure that his customers are completely satisfied. In addition to providing expert window tinting services, Lowkey Window Tint is also committed to giving back to the community. We believe that it's important to support the place where we live and work, and we are always looking for ways to contribute. If you're looking for top-quality window tinting services that are backed by a personal touch and a commitment to customer satisfaction, look no further than Lowkey Window Tint.
